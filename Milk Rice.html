Milk Rice

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1 - Rice
  • Ingredient 2 - Coconut Milk
  • Ingredient 3 - Chilie

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: wash and steam rice for 15 mins
  2. Step 2: Add Coconut milk & boil it for 20 mins
  3. Step 3: Then enjoy it with chilie .
