Hoppers

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1 - Flour
  • Ingredient 2 - water
  • Ingredient 3 - Yeast
  • Ingredient 4 - Coconut milk

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water together
  2. Step 2: Add the yeast mixture to rice flour and salt.
  3. Step 3: Add coconut milk to the mixture.
  4. Step 4: Cover the bowl and let rise.
  5. Step 5: Heat a pan over medium heat.
  6. Step 6: Add a small amount of oil to the pan.
  7. Step 7: Add a ladle-full of batter and swirl it around the pan.
  8. Step 8: Remove from the pan carefully and enjoy.
